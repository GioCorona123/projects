Reveiw of the show Adventure Time

★★★★★

Gio Corona, january 29, 2024

Adventure Time, created by Pendleton Ward, is an animated series that follows the main characters
of Finn the Human and Jake the Dog in the Land of Ooo. This show combines a bright cartoony
style, a wide range of humor, and extensive lore, to appeal to audiences of all ages. With a hefty
cast of characters including Finn, Jake, Princess Bubblegum, Ice King, Marceline, and many many
more, Adventure Time explores themes of adventure and self-discovery in a post-apocalyptic setting.
The extensive series length, clever character development, and a unique approach to storytelling
make it my favorite series that I've ever watched. Over its ten seasons, Adventure Time has become
a staple of my childhood and many others and is praised for its creativity and emotional depth.

trivia about Adventure Time

  • trivia #1
    The post-apocalyptic Earth in Adventure Time has an enormous hole in its side, caused by a war (known as the Mushroom War), waged around 1,000 years before the series's main timeline.
  • trivia #2
    As a running gag, in every episode (except in Food Chain (2014)), a small snail is in frame, waving at viewers. The snail is usually only on screen for one shot per episode.
  • trivia #3
    With 10 seasons, this is the most seasons for a Cartoon Network show. It's the longest running Cartoon Network show with 8 years.
